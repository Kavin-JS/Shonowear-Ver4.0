PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy matters. Here is exactly how we handle your data.

Last updated: January 2025

WHAT WE COLLECT

When you shop on Shonowear, we collect only what we need to process your order and improve your experience:

  • Name and email address when you create an account or sign up for our newsletter
  • Delivery address and phone number when you place an order
  • Browsing behaviour on our site to improve product recommendations
  • Cart and wishlist data stored locally on your device

HOW WE USE IT

  • To process and ship your orders
  • To send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • To notify you of new drops if you have subscribed (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To improve our product range and site experience

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

COOKIES

We use minimal cookies to keep your session active and remember your cart. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect your shopping experience.

DATA SECURITY

Your data is stored securely. Cart and wishlist data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage and never transmitted to our servers without your action.

YOUR RIGHTS

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Ask us to delete your account and associated data
  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@shonowear.com
