SHIPPING POLICY

Fast, reliable delivery across India.

Last updated: January 2025

DELIVERY TIMES

  • Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune): 2–4 business days
  • Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities: 4–7 business days
  • Remote areas: 7–10 business days

All orders are dispatched within 24 hours of placement on business days.

SHIPPING CHARGES

  • Orders above ₹999 — FREE shipping
  • Orders below ₹999 — ₹49 flat shipping fee
  • Express delivery (1–2 days) — ₹99 additional, available in select cities

TRACKING YOUR ORDER

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can track your order in real time through our logistics partner's portal.

FAILED DELIVERY

If delivery is attempted and fails (no one available, incorrect address), our courier will attempt redelivery twice. After two failed attempts, the order is returned to us. Contact us to rearrange delivery or request a refund.

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING

We currently ship within India only. International shipping is planned for a future release. Join our newsletter to be notified when it launches.
