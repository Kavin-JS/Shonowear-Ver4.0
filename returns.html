RETURNS POLICY

Not happy with your order? We make returns easy.

Last updated: January 2025

RETURN WINDOW

You can return any item within 7 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange, provided the item is unused, unwashed, and in its original packaging with all tags attached.

ELIGIBLE ITEMS

  • Hoodies, tees, jackets, and oversized clothing — eligible for return or exchange
  • Phone covers — eligible if unopened and unused
  • Figurines and collectibles — eligible only if damaged or defective on arrival

Items that have been worn, washed, or damaged after delivery are not eligible for return.

HOW TO RETURN

  • Email support@shonowear.com with your order details and reason for return
  • We will send you a return shipping label within 24 hours
  • Pack the item securely and drop it off at the nearest courier partner
  • Refund is processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the return

EXCHANGES

Want a different size? We offer free size exchanges on all apparel within 7 days. Just mention the size you need in your return email and we will ship the replacement immediately.

DAMAGED OR WRONG ITEM

If you received a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We will replace or refund at no cost to you, including return shipping.

CONTACT

Returns questions? Email support@shonowear.com or call +91 98765 43210 between 10am–7pm IST, Mon–Sat.
